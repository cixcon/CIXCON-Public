How we work

Four principles we don't compromise on

Consulting firms say a lot of things. These are the four we've staked the practice on.

Clarity

We say what we actually think. The answer first, the reasoning second. If something is uncertain, we say so plainly. Most organizational problems get worse when people avoid the direct statement. We don't.

Accountability

When Class IX makes a recommendation, we stand behind it fully, through implementation, adjustment, and completion. Our engagement doesn't end when the presentation does.

Ownership

Our job is to solve your problem, not to create dependency. We design solutions you can operate, sustain, and build on long after we're gone. No unnecessary software ties, no inflated scope, no manufactured reasons to keep us around. We own the outcome. You own the deliverable.

Partnership

We are as invested in solving your problem as you are. That means getting hands-on, working inside the details, and staying engaged until the work is actually done. You know your organization better than we do. We count on that. What we bring is the outside perspective and structured thinking that turns your knowledge into a solution.

Founder & Principal
Brian Wallis

Brian Wallis founded Class IX Consulting to solve the specific problem a client actually has, not the broader engagement most firms would rather sell. The work stays targeted, moves the business forward, and doesn't add overhead it doesn't need.

He brings seven years of active duty service as a U.S. Army Engineer Officer, a B.S. in Civil Engineering from the University of South Carolina, and an MBA from the University of Louisville, earned while in command of a logistics company at Fort Knox, Kentucky. That combination of technical training, graduate-level business education, and operational leadership under pressure is the foundation Class IX is built on.

Since transitioning focus to the private sector, Brian has delivered engagements across strategy, operations, logistics, digital transformation, and leadership development for commercial and public-sector clients. He holds certifications in Project Management and Managerial Analytics, brings deep experience in Generative and Agentic AI, and maintains an active Secret Security Clearance.

Based in Charleston, South Carolina.
