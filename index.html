How we work

Four principles we don't compromise on

Consulting firms say a lot of things. These are the four we've staked the practice on.

01

Clarity

We say what we actually think. The answer first, the reasoning second. If something is uncertain, we say so plainly. Most organizational problems get worse when people avoid the direct statement — we don't.

02

Accountability

We own the outcome, not just the deliverable. When CIX makes a recommendation, we stand behind it fully — through implementation, adjustment, and completion. Our engagement doesn't end when the presentation does.

03

Ownership

Our job is to solve your problem — not to create dependency. We design solutions you can operate, sustain, and build on long after we're gone. No unnecessary software ties, no inflated scope, no manufactured reasons to keep us around. When the work is done, it belongs to you.

04

Partnership

We are as invested in solving your problem as you are. That means getting hands-on, working inside the details, and staying engaged until the work is actually done. You know your organization better than we do — we count on that. What we bring is the outside perspective and structured thinking that turns your knowledge into a solution.

Engagements that moved the needle

Founder & Principal
Brian Wallis
Founder, Class IX Consulting

Brian Wallis founded Class IX Consulting on a straightforward premise: the gap between where a company is and where it could be is almost always smaller than it looks. CIX meets clients where they are, solves the problems that are actually holding them back, and delivers compact, targeted work designed to drive the business forward without adding excessive overhead.

He brings seven years of active duty service as a U.S. Army Engineer Officer, a B.S. in Civil Engineering from the University of South Carolina, and an MBA from the University of Louisville — earned while in command of a logistics company at Fort Knox, Kentucky. That combination of technical training, graduate-level business education, and operational leadership under pressure is the foundation CIX is built on.

Since transitioning to the private sector, Brian has delivered engagements across strategy, operations, digital transformation, and leadership development for commercial, nonprofit, and public-sector clients. He holds certifications in Project Management, Generative AI Integration, and Managerial Analytics, and maintains an active Secret Security Clearance.

Based in Charleston, South Carolina.
